The Cera Store

About
The original Cera ceramics store was started by my grandmother in 1934 in Massachusetts. To continue the family tradition that began in her quaint brick & mortar shop, we created this online store to share her exquisite designs with the whole world! All of our products are handmade in our local workshop just a few blocks from the original Cera store. We hope you find something you love!
